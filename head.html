About the Project

The Multimodal RAG System is an innovative solution that combines retrieval-augmented generation (RAG) with multimodal data processing. It is designed to analyze and generate answers using text, images, and audio, making it ideal for applications in education, healthcare, and research. By integrating advanced AI technologies, we aim to make knowledge more accessible and contextualized for everyone.
